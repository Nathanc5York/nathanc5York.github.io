What is Eco-Life?

Eco-Life is a website aimed towards making the planet more green, one step at a time.
We will help track your progress to making the earth a more eco-friendly world with the multiple tools we provide.

You can use a Solar Panel Calculator to estimate the ROI on solar panels built,
and we can show you the difference normal everyday objects and eco-friendly options.
