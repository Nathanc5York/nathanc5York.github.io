Environmental Comparisons

Gasoline vs. Electricity

A study[1] in 2019 compared the CO2 emissions gasoline and electric cars produce.

They studied the different models that car manufacturers produced and found that over a car's lifetime, the electric car produced 57% less CO2 per mile.

They even found that in fossil fuel powered states, an electric car still beat out a gasoline car in terms of CO2 sustainability.
